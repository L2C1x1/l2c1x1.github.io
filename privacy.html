Information we collect
  1. Information you provide during account registration:
    1. *username
    2. *password hash
    3. security questions (2)
    4. MD5 checksum of the security answers (2)
    5. name
    6. email
    7. phone number
    8. mobile phone number
    9. registration date
    10. zip code
    11. address
    Only fields marked with asterisks (*) are mandatory. We do not store the password itself.
  2. IP addresses you use to access our servers and services
  3. Your Facebook ID ‐ if you register using our chatbot m.me/l2c1x1
  4. In-game data, including character actions and chats
How do we store it
  1. Unencrypted, with restricted access:
    1. Registration information
    2. In-game data
  2. Via third-party services (their corresponding privacy policies apply):
    1. Emails via Gmail
    2. Messages in social networks via VK and FB
What do we share
  1. We do not share any information we collect with any third party.
  2. Some information is stored using third-party services (see previous section), their corresponding privacy policies apply.
